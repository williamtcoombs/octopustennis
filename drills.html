Training Drills

Sharpen consistency, decision-making, and fitness through focused, game-like repetitions. Every ball has a purpose.


Foundation Drills

Build reliable control before layering speed or pressure.

  • Cross-Court Groove (5 mins): Rally diagonally, aim waist height, count consecutive balls. Switch sides.
  • Middle Line Rally: Two players rally deep down the center to train margin and recovery.
  • Target Box: Drop cones at deep corners; 10-ball challenge to each box.

Competitive Sets

Add scoring pressure and accountability to practice. Every drill here trains focus under match-like stress.

  • 21 Rally: First to 21 consecutive rally points. Miss = -1, winner = +2.
  • Serve Race: First to 10 made first serves into targets wins.
  • Point Ladder: 3 points in a row = move up a court; lose 3 = move down.

Fitness & Footwork Integration

Pair movement discipline with technical reps. Train quick recovery and balanced rhythm.

  • Split-Step + Recover: 10-second bursts shadowing forehand and backhand patterns.
  • Shadow Rally: Diagonal footwork, 20-ball simulation, stay light on toes.
  • Box Step Ladders: Sprint between cones mimicking court coverage angles.
