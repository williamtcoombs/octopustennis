Fitness

Strengthen your game from the ground up with agility, endurance, and focus.

Tennis Fitness

Train your body to match your game. Strength, agility, and endurance power every shot and recovery.


Movement & Agility

Explosive first steps and smooth recovery win rallies before the swing begins.

  • Split-Step Repeats: 3×20 sec rounds; react to visual cues or coach calls.
  • Lateral Shuffle Ladder: 4 cones; shuffle right/left for 20 sec, rest 10 sec.
  • Recovery Races: From doubles alley → hash mark → baseline, repeat 5×.

Strength & Stability

Build strong, balanced movements to stay powerful and injury-resistant.

  • Core Rotations: 3×12 per side with medicine ball or band.
  • Single-Leg Balance Swings: 3×10 per leg; stay stable and control follow-through.
  • Lunge Matrix: Forward, diagonal, and side lunge sequence; maintain upright posture.

Endurance & Conditioning

Sustain your best tennis for every point—train recovery, rhythm, and breath control.

  • Baseline Intervals: Sprint corner-to-corner for 20 seconds, rest 20, repeat ×8.
  • Serve Recovery Runs: Serve, recover to hash, side shuffle, repeat ×10 per side.
  • Point Builder Circuit: 30 sec forehand shadow → 30 sec backhand → 30 sec rest ×4 rounds.

Layer these intervals with on-court rally work from the Drills page.

Flexibility & Recovery

Protect joints and enhance movement efficiency through consistent mobility work.

  • Dynamic Warm-Up: Leg swings, arm circles, side lunges.
  • Post-Practice Stretch: Hamstrings, hip flexors, shoulders, back.
  • Mobility Routine: 5-minute cooldown focused on hips and thoracic rotation.

Consistency beats intensity—finish every session with recovery work.

Mental & Team Fitness

Confidence grows when body and mind move together. Train focus, calm, and connection.

  • Visualization: Picture 3 points played with perfect footwork and breathing.
  • Team Relays: Sprint, tag, recover—compete together with energy and laughter.
  • Mindful Cooldown: Slow breathing and reflection between rounds or sets.

Readiness Checklist

  • ⬜ Explosive first step in every drill
  • ⬜ Balanced recovery after movement
  • ⬜ Completed cooldown stretch
  • ⬜ Logged progress and next focus

Strength and control today fuel confidence tomorrow. Keep stacking small wins.
