Quick Start: Serve

Start every point with purpose. Build a reliable routine and +1 plan.

  • Ritual, balance, high contact
  • Targets: wide, body, T
  • Pressure plan: body-first, breathe, big margins

Quick Start: Return

Steal time back with a compact swing and deep middle target.

  • Split on contact; read toss/shoulders
  • Targets: deep middle, cross, surprise line
  • Neutral first, attack second

This Week’s Focus

“Deep middle wins neutral.” In practice and matches, aim 3–4 feet over the net strap and recover inside the hash.

  • ✔️ 10-Target Serve Challenge
  • ✔️ Return Deep Middle (block or drive)
  • ✔️ 1–2 Punch: heavy cross → open lane
  • ✔️ Fitness: split-step + recover rounds

Rotate this weekly message as your team emphasis changes.
