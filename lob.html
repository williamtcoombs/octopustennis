Lob

Lift under pressure with balance, height, and intent.

What the Lob Does

The lob buys time, resets when you’re pressured, and punishes opponents who camp the net. Your goals: get height, get depth, and recover. If they smash, you make them hit one more ball from an awkward spot; if they retreat, you take the court back.

Fundamentals

Setup & Footwork

  • Stance: Wide base, chest up, chin level. Think “lift with legs.”
  • Contact: Slightly in front, strings facing the sky; finish high over shoulder.
  • Margin: Aim 10–15 ft above net tape (~3–4× net height) on defensive lobs.

Contact & Finish

  • Low → High: Brush up, not across. Let the racket “carry” the ball up.
  • Head still: Freeze after contact for one beat; watch the ball up the line it travels.
  • Recover: Split-step as the ball peaks; read their movement (retreat vs. smash).

Common Mistakes

  • Trying to “place” with the wrist (causes floaters). Use legs + forearm, not wrist flicks.
  • Hitting too flat (low arc). Build height first; depth follows.
  • Watching the result instead of recovering. Finish, split, and reposition immediately.

Types of Lobs

Defensive Lob

When stretched or under pace: big arc, big margin, middle of court. Reset the point.

  • Target: Deep middle to avoid angles.
  • Cue: “Lift the sky,” finish high, recover to center.

Topspin Lob

When they’re close to the net and you have time: fast lift with heavy brush so it dips in.

  • Target: Cross or DTL over their backhand side.
  • Cue: “Brush the back up,” accelerate through contact.

Slice / Touch Lob

Use when they’re backpedaling already: carve under slightly; soft hands, early prep.

Volley & On-the-Run Lobs

Short backswing, firm wrist on the volley lob; for on-the-run, exaggerate lift and clear higher.

Patterns (Singles & Doubles)

Singles

  • vs. Net-Rushers: Deep cross defensive lob → recover → attack next short ball.
  • Pull & Lob: Roll heavy cross to pull them wide, then topspin lob DTL.

Doubles

  • Over the Poacher: Topspin lob over active net player; follow to middle.
  • Reset Middle: Defensive lob down the center to neutralize both players.

Drills (Measurable Goals)

Arc & Depth Ladder

Cones at baseline center. Aim to land balls past the cones with a high arc.

  • Goal: 8/10 balls clear net by ≥12 ft and land past the baseline cone.

Topspin Dip Challenge

Partner at service line. Hit topspin lobs that clear high and dip inside court.

  • Goal: 6/10 drop inside last 6 feet with visible topspin kick.

Pressure Reset

Coach feeds on the run. Player must lift a defensive lob, recover, and split on peak.

  • Goal: 5 successful resets in a row (height + recovery timing).

Poacher Counter (Doubles)

Opponent poaches; you respond with lob over their backhand shoulder.

  • Goal: 7/10 land deep cross; partner pinches middle on your call.
