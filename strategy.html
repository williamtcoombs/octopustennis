Strategy

Play smart: big margins, simple choices, relentless consistency — then strike when you’ve earned it.

Strategy

This is your decision compass. It turns chaos into simple priorities so you can play calm, clear, and committed.

Coach’s Cue: “Plan → Breathe → Commit.” If you don’t have a plan, pick deep middle and reset.

Core Pillars

  • Percentages: Big targets, net clearance, spin. Miss long; never in the tape.
  • Pressure: Build height and depth first; finish once you’ve moved the opponent.
  • Patience: Stay in patterns; win boring. Make them play one more quality ball.
  • Position: Recover inside the hash. Own the middle until you’ve earned the angle.

Standard: 65–70% of points started with a high‑margin target (deep middle or body).

Between‑Point Routine (BPR)

  1. Plan: Call target + purpose (build / pressure / finish).
  2. Breathe: 4‑count inhale, 6‑count exhale; loosen the jaw/shoulders.
  3. Commit: Cue word at bounce (“Height” / “Middle” / “Up”).

If doubt creeps in, default to deep middle with height and reset your feet.

Scoreboard Rules

  • Up 30–0 / 40–15: Pressure with depth; earn angles on ball two.
  • 30–30 / Deuce: Highest margin targets only: deep middle or body.
  • Break Points (for or against): Serve/return to the big target you trust most.
  • Tie‑breakers: First ball height; no hero shots off balance.

Target: Hold serve 75%+ by playing first‑ball to margin.

Environment & Adjustments

  • Wind: Into wind → drive more; with wind → add height and spin.
  • Sun: Favor cross‑court lobs and higher net clearance into glare.
  • Surface: Slow → extend patterns; Fast → simplify to first‑strike + big targets.

Standard: 0 unforced tape errors in the game after a side change.

Strategic Game Plans

Serve‑First Plan

Serve to big targets; play first ball to deep middle; finish to space.

Goal: Win 65–70% of serve+1 exchanges.

See Tactics for Serve+1 trees.

Return‑First Plan

Neutralize height to the body or deep middle; step in on seconds.

Goal: Win 45% of return games by forcing a short ball in the first three shots.

See Tactics for Return+1 branches.

Neutral to Offense

Live cross‑court until you see short / middle / height, then take space.

Cue: “Short or middle? Go.”

Defense to Reset

Raise height, play deep middle, recover inside the hash; buy time.

Goal: Reset to neutral within two balls 6/10 times.

Opponent Archetypes

  • Big Hitter: Add height and depth; make them hit one more from uncomfortable positions.
  • Counter‑puncher: Take early inside the court; finish to space, not the line.
  • Net Rusher: Body/feet targets first; lob or pass only when balanced.
  • All‑Court: Win the middle; vary height and tempo to force decisions.

The detailed playbook lives on Tactics.

Match Metrics & Review

  • First‑Ball Margin: 80%+ of first balls clear net by 2–3 feet.
  • Serve+1 Win%: 65–70% target in practice sets.
  • Neutral Rally Wins: 60% under 6 balls; 55% over 6 balls.
  • Emotional Errors: ≤1 rushed, low‑margin swing per set.

Post‑Match: Note one principle you kept and one you’ll emphasize next match.

Strategy Checklist

  • ⬜ Big targets with height on first ball
  • ⬜ Recover inside the hash after every swing
  • ⬜ Decide purpose before serve/return (build / pressure / finish)
  • ⬜ BPR every point: Plan → Breathe → Commit

Weekly Challenge: Play two practice sets keeping a written BPR on the towel; track Serve+1 win% and first‑ball margin.
