Strategy

Make smart choices under pressure: simple priorities, trusted patterns, and a clear plan between points.

Match Strategy

Strategy is the bridge between your skills and the score. When in doubt: play deep to the middle, change direction only on balanced balls, and attack short balls with margin.

Singles Shot Selection Matrix

Quick reference for patterns and choices by situation.

Shot choices by situation with risk and coaching cues
Situation Preferred Shot Alternative Risk Cue / Notes
Neutral rally (deep middle) Deep cross-court topspin Heavy neutral ball middle Low Height over net; big target
Short ball inside baseline Aggressive approach to corner Deep topspin middle Medium Commit early; follow to net
Defensive scramble High, deep lob or slice Moonball middle Low Reset the rally; buy time

Tip: On mobile, scroll horizontally to view all columns.

Principles

  • High-percentage targets: cross-court and deep middle.
  • Pattern first, power later: earn the line before changing direction.
  • Margin: aim higher on defense; depth over speed.
