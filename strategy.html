Strategy

Build smarter points. Shape rallies with intent. Control the flow of every match.

Building a Strategic Mindset

Strategy in tennis is about structure and anticipation — crafting points that play to your strengths while exposing your opponent’s weaknesses.

Understanding Patterns

Great players win not because they hit harder, but because they hit smarter. Recognize recurring situations: crosscourt exchanges, short balls, deep rally patterns. Identify what shot combinations produce the highest percentage of success for you.

Adapting Mid-Match

A strong strategy adjusts. Recognize early when a plan isn’t working, and have a backup ready. Change direction, pace, or positioning to disrupt rhythm.

Energy Management

Strategy includes pacing — knowing when to press and when to reset. Use efficient movement and shot selection to conserve stamina and stay fresh deep into matches.
