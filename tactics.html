Turn principles into patterns. One simple choice at a time — with metrics.

Tactics

This is your applied playbook. Use the frameworks below to make fast, high‑percentage choices under pressure.

Coach’s Cue: “Plan → Breathe → Commit.” From Strategy.

Serve +1 Trees

Deuce Court

Pick one pattern per game; change if they solve it.
  • Wide → +1 to open court (safe to deep corner).
  • Body → +1 deep middle; look for short ball next.
  • T‑serve → +1 inside‑out; hold the line only when set.

Metric: Win 65–70% of serve+1 points.

Ad Court

Highest margin on big points.
  • Body → +1 deep middle; shrink angles first.
  • Wide kicker → +1 to space; follow to net if floated.
  • T‑serve → +1 cross then inside‑in to finish.

Metric: First ball clears net by 2–3 ft 80%+.

Use your strength early; repeat until they prove a counter.

Return +1 Schemes

Vs First Serve

  • Block middle/body; height beats pace.
  • +1 deep middle to reset, then take space.

Metric: Neutralize 7/10 first serves in play.

Vs Second Serve

  • Step in; pick forehand if possible.
  • Drive to body/deep middle; follow to the ball.

Metric: Create a short ball within 3 shots 60%+.

Neutral Patterns

  • Live cross‑court until you see short / middle / height — then change.
  • Height first on rally ball one; raise net clearance when nervous.
  • Inside‑in only when set; otherwise recycle to deep middle.

Target: Win 60% of neutral rallies under 6 balls.

Transition & Net (Singles)

  • Approach to deep middle unless the angle is obvious.
  • First volley: deep middle; second to angle or body jam.
  • Read → Volleys: standard to build, drop to punish, lob volley to reset.

Cue: “Middle first.” Earn the sharp angle on ball two.

Doubles: Formations & Signals

Serve Formations

  • Standard: Poach on predictable returns; middle first on big points.
  • I‑Formation: Hide weakness; signal poach/stay; serve body.
  • Australian: Take returner’s strength; guard the line with the server.

Metric: Hold 80% of service games across the team.

Return Teams

  • Middle first to jam poacher and shrink angles.
  • Lob return over active net player 1–2 times per set.

Metric: Break 1 in 3 games at 3.5–4.0 level; adjust by level.

Use simple hand signals; confirm the if/then before every point.

Opponent‑Type Plans

Big Hitter

  • If they blast cross → then raise height and play deep middle.
  • If they rush net → then jam body; lob only when balanced.

Metric: Force 2+ extra balls before you change direction.

Counter‑puncher

  • If they float short → then approach deep middle and follow.
  • If rallies stretch long → then step inside on neutral balls.

Metric: Finish within 2 shots after gaining court.

Net Rusher

  • If they close early → then return/body jam; pass to space on ball two.
  • If you’re jammed → then lob volley or lift high middle to reset.

Metric: Win 60% of points when they touch first volley.

All‑Court

  • If they vary tempo → then anchor to deep middle, change on your terms.
  • If they poach often → then return middle and lob once per game.

Metric: Keep unforced tape errors ≤1 per set.

Pressure Packages

  • Serve (Ad): Body or T → +1 deep middle. No line‑painting.
  • Return (Deuce): Block middle; +1 deep middle; reset feet.
  • Rally: Height + depth until you see a sitter; then attack to space.

Goal: Win 55%+ of deuce/ad deciding points.

Special Situations

  • Wind: Into wind → drive more; with wind → add height & spin; aim bigger targets.
  • Sun/Glare: Favor cross‑court lobs and higher net clearance.
  • Fatigue: Play middle first, shorten swings, walk the towel (BPR).

Drill Bridges

  • Serve+1 Ladder: Repeat chosen tree ×10 each side; track win%.
  • Return Jam: Block middle → +1 deep middle; score only on pressure points.
  • Deep Middle → Angle: Two‑ball pattern from Volley.

Tactics Checklist

  • ⬜ One pattern per game (don’t sample endlessly)
  • ⬜ First ball to margin (height + big target)
  • ⬜ Call the if/then before pressure points
  • ⬜ BPR every point: Plan → Breathe → Commit

Weekly Challenge: Track Serve+1 and Return+1 win% across two practice sets; aim for +10% improvement week over week.
