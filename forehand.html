Forehand

Build your shot from the ground up — solid stance, early unit turn, smooth acceleration, full follow-through.

Forehand Fundamentals

  • Turn Early: Rotate shoulders before contact — racquet back smoothly.
  • Contact in Front: Meet the ball just ahead of the body line for control.
  • Finish High: Follow through fully — palm toward target.

A repeatable forehand starts with timing, tempo, and trust.

Drills for Consistency

  • Shadow Swings: 10 reps with balanced finish.
  • Target Practice: Hit 10 forehands aiming for depth, not pace.
  • Rally Tempo: Rally 20 balls at 60% — focus on rhythm.

Small, consistent reps build confidence under pressure.
